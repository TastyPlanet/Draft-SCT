attr可以设置任何属性:
<form action="subscribe.html"
    th:attr="action=@{/subscribe}"
    method="POST">
若设置多个属性,需要用逗号隔开:
<img src="image.png"
   th:attr="src=@{/images/logo.png},
    title=#{logo},
    alt=#{logo}"/>
